# Privacy Policy for Logic Gate Glow Lab

Logic Gate Glow Lab ("the Game") is a logic puzzle game published by Neon Games ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Game.

## Information Stored on Your Device

The Game stores the following information locally on your device:

- Game progress and settings
- Tutorial completion state
- Saved Free Build circuit names and circuit data
- Auto Build credit state
- Whether a valid no-ads promotional code has been activated

This local information is used only to provide the Game's features. We do not operate user accounts, and this information is not sent to our own servers.

You can delete locally stored information by clearing the Game's storage in Android settings or uninstalling the Game. Deleting the information may reset progress, settings, saved circuits, and earned benefits.

## Advertising and Third-Party Data

The Game uses Google AdMob to provide optional rewarded advertisements. If you choose to watch a rewarded advertisement, Google and its advertising partners may automatically collect or process information such as:

- Device and advertising identifiers
- IP address and general location derived from it
- Device, operating system, and app information
- Advertisement views, interactions, and reward events
- Diagnostic, security, and fraud-prevention information

Depending on your location, consent choices, device settings, and Google's policies, advertisements may be personalized, non-personalized, limited, or used only for technical ad delivery. Google acts according to its own privacy policy and terms.

The Game also uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request and manage privacy choices where required. Available privacy choices can be reviewed through the privacy options presented in the Game or on your device, when applicable.

For more information, see:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)
- [Google Ads controls](https://myadcenter.google.com/)

## Android Permissions

The Game may use internet and network-state permissions to load advertisements and communicate with Google advertising services. Google Mobile Ads may use the Android Advertising ID permission on supported Android versions. The Game does not request access to your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or precise location.

## How Information Is Used

Information handled through the Game and its advertising provider may be used to:

- Provide and maintain gameplay features
- Save progress and user preferences locally
- Show and measure optional rewarded advertisements
- Grant rewards after completed advertisements
- Prevent advertising fraud and protect service security
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by Google and its advertising partners when advertising services are used. Their handling of information is governed by their respective privacy policies and user consent choices.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Local game data remains on your device until you clear the Game's storage or uninstall it. Advertising information is retained by Google according to Google's policies and your account, consent, and device settings.

Because the Game does not provide accounts or upload local save data to our servers, we cannot remotely identify or delete local game data. You can delete it through **Android Settings > Apps > Logic Gate Glow Lab > Storage > Clear storage**, or by uninstalling the Game.

## Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service used by the Game, contact us so we can help direct the request to the appropriate provider.

## International Users

Advertising providers may process information in countries other than the country where you live. Their privacy policies describe the safeguards and legal bases they use for international processing.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the Game, its services, or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted at the same public URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
